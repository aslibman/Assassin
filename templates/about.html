Assassin

About

Assassin is a game created by Kyler Chase, Alex Libman, Julian Kalogerkis, Victor Gaitor, and David Dvorkin. The web app is based off the traditional assassin game in which players are assigned targets randomly and must "take out" their target using a predetermined method of assassination. In our version, the assassin must take a picture of their target and upload it. Our face recognition software matches photos for kills, allows geolocation tracking, and all player stats are kept track of in their profile, revolutionizing the traditional game. To get started, all you need to do is create an account and make or join a match.
